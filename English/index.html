MTM Analysis & Interactive Productivity Management Systems

We provide innovative solutions for sustainable growth in
The Garment Industry!

From Operation Analysis To Cost...

In the design phase, the operations of the models are analyzed by the MTM method and the Standard Minute Values (SMV) of the production processes are calculated and an institutional costing standard is created.

From Line Balance to Operation and Model Planning...

After orders are routed to the relevant production units and lines, line optimization is done according to the minute capacities of the production lines. With reference to line optimization, model planning is created in the detail of the operation.

From Model Planning To Production Follow-Up...

Instant production data, quality indicators and machine lost times of the operators working on the lines are monitored.

From Employee Efficiency to "Win & Win" Model...

"Win Win" model is established with bonus system by analyzing employees productivity.

Dynamic Reports from Analysis to Production Management...

Instant dynamic reports with Planned/Actual comparisons.
